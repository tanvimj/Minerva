FAQ

Questions, answered

  • Is my data private and secure? ↓

    Yes. All your notes and data are encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell your data or use it to train models. You own everything you create in Minerva.

  • Can I import from other apps like Notion or Obsidian? ↓

    Absolutely. Minerva supports import from Markdown, Notion exports, and plain text. We're actively building deeper integrations — more coming soon.

  • Does Minerva work offline? ↓

    Yes — the desktop and mobile apps support full offline mode. Your notes sync automatically when you reconnect. Nothing is lost if your connection drops.

  • What makes the Knowledge Graph different? ↓

    Most graph tools require you to manually draw connections. Minerva does it automatically — surfacing relationships between your notes, goals, and projects as you work. You can also add manual links whenever you want.

  • Can I cancel my subscription at any time? ↓

    Yes, always. No lock-in, no cancellation fees. If you cancel, you keep access until the end of your billing period. Your data is yours to export at any time.

  • Is there a mobile app? ↓

    Mobile apps for iOS and Android are currently in beta. Web access works great on mobile browsers in the meantime. Sign up to be notified when the apps launch.
